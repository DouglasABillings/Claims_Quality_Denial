# 🏥 Claims Quality & Denial Analytics Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

Healthcare organizations process thousands of insurance claims, making it difficult to quickly identify denial trends, reimbursement performance, and data quality issues that delay payment.

This Power BI dashboard consolidates key claims metrics into a single reporting solution, allowing operations teams to monitor financial performance, identify claims requiring attention, and prioritize remediation efforts.

The dashboard is organized into three reporting pages that provide an executive summary, denial analytics, and claims quality auditing.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall claims performance.
- Track reimbursement and denial rates.
- Identify the most common denial reasons.
- Surface claims requiring follow-up.
- Highlight data quality issues impacting the claims process.
- Provide an interactive reporting tool for operational review.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Executive Overview

- Processed **1,000 total claims**.
- Overall reimbursement rate of **63.3%**.
- Overall audit flag rate of **37.3%**.
- Revenue funnel compares billed, allowed, and paid amounts.
- Monthly claims volume highlights fluctuations in claim activity.

### Denial Analytics

- Overall denial rate of **32.7%**.
- Denial reasons are ranked to quickly identify the most common causes of claim denials.
- AR Status distribution provides visibility into the current claims lifecycle.
- Claims denied without an associated reason code are surfaced for additional review.

### Claims Quality Audit

The audit page focuses on operational issues that may require manual intervention, including:

- Status and outcome conflicts
- Underpayment flags
- Missing outcome values
- Missing denial reason codes
- Payer name corrections
- Claims requiring follow-up

Together these metrics help prioritize remediation efforts and identify opportunities to improve claims data quality.
